Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: June 03, 2025

What is Nexus Forum?

Nexus Forum is a community platform for space enthusiasts to discuss astronomy, space exploration, and related topics.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the navigation menu and follow the registration process.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password" to receive reset instructions via email.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting our support team.

What are the posting rules?

We encourage respectful discussions. Any form of harassment, spam, or inappropriate content will be removed.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Each post has a "Report" button that you can use to flag content for moderator review.
